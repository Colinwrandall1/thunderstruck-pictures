Writer · Director · Producer

Martin Shapiro

Wrote, directed, and produced the horror short Chopper, from his own comic series — its prequel became a web series starring Tyler Mane (Halloween, X-Men). His thriller Lair of the Fox sold to Ilya Salkind (producer of Superman).

He's developed for HBO and MGM, including an adaptation of Dragonlance. MFA in Screenwriting, UCLA — where he won the Showcase award for his war drama Crossfire.

Director · Director of Photography

Taylor Randall

Music videos for Jennifer Lopez, Cardi B, Bad Bunny, DJ Khaled, Nipsey Hussle, John Legend, Enrique Iglesias, Becky G.

From Chicago to LA at 17, Taylor learned the set from every seat. Twelve-plus years in, he's one of the rare few who directs and shoots at once — for Kodak Black, Sunny Malouf, Tahj Mowry, Dizzy Wright, and Mann.
