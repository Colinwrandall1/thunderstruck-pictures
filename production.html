Writer · Director · Producer

Martin Shapiro

Wrote, directed, and produced the horror short Chopper, from his own comic series — its prequel became a web series starring Tyler Mane (Halloween, X-Men). His thriller Lair of the Fox sold to Ilya Salkind (producer of Superman).

He's developed for HBO and MGM, including an adaptation of Dragonlance. MFA in Screenwriting, UCLA — where he won the Showcase award for his war drama Crossfire.

Director of Photography

Taylor Randall

A union director of photography — IATSE Local 600 for more than 19 years. Taylor's credits run across feature films, network TV series, global brands, and some of the biggest artists in the world.

From Chicago to LA at 17, he learned the set from every seat — and has since shot for Cardi B, Justin Bieber, Bad Bunny, The Weeknd, and Eminem, with work for Netflix and BET+.
