How to choose

Three things to weigh
before you pick.

Who walks in first?

If most visitors are Austin grip crews looking for gear, lean cooler (V2 Cinematic) or warmer (V3 Texan). If most visitors are festival programmers or financiers checking on Chopper, V1 Electric or V4 Horror earns its keep faster.

What do the photos look like?

The truck photo grades easily into all four. The Chopper poster is already crimson-leaning — pairs strongest with V4 Horror, fights a little with V3 Texan's amber. Future gear photography should be planned around the choice.

Future-proofing.

V1 Electric is the "true to logo" choice — anything else means the existing mascot logo and the new brand are slightly different conversations. Not a problem, but worth naming.
