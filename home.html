The 3-Ton Grip Truck

The rig that runs the show.

The perfect mix of capacity and mobility — built to streamline load-ins and load-outs. Modular shelving and all-terrain carts keep crews nimble, with a built-in camera workstation, 15 outlets, and shore power for charging on set.

  • Jockey boxesCustom under-truck boxes for sandbags, stingers, and grip — each one lit so you're never fumbling in the dark.
